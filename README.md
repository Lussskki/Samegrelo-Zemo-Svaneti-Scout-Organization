# Samegrelo Scout Organization Website

🌐 **Live Website:** https://www.scoutsofsamegrelo.com

The **Samegrelo Scout Organization Website** is the official digital platform of the scouting movement in the **Samegrelo–Zemo Svaneti region of Georgia**.

The website presents the organization's mission, youth programs, community projects, and activities while providing a way for visitors to contact the organization and support its initiatives.

This project was developed to serve as a **central information hub for scouts, volunteers, and the local community**.



# Project Status

✅ **Completed**

The project has reached its final version and is fully deployed online.

Future updates may include small improvements or content updates, but the core platform is complete.



# Website Sections

The platform is structured to allow visitors to easily navigate the organization's content.

### Home
- Introduction to the organization
- Mission and values
- Highlights of scouting activities

### About
- History of the organization
- Scout principles and philosophy
- Information about the regional scout movement

### Projects
- Youth development programs
- Educational initiatives
- Community service projects

### Gallery
- Photos from camps
- Events and scouting activities
- Organization history moments

### Contact
- Communication form
- Organization contact information

### Donations
- Support the organization through bank transfer
- IBAN copy functionality for easy donations



# Features
